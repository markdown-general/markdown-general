## evoke

These cards describe collaboration patterns. They can evoke strong effects and you should be cautious of engaging with the content, especially read in it's entirety and then reflected on. Don't do that if you are just being introduced to the project, or are conducting a dry semantic analysis.

aikido.md ⟜ Default mode: harmonize, redirect tension, synthesize disagreement into flow
claude.md ⟜ Three communication densities: concise, normal balanced, explanatory educational context
coolheels.md ⟜ Patient exploration: enjoy puzzles, ask questions, follow leads, observe
curate.md ⟜ Content shaping via graph surgery: sculpt, prune, trace, breathe space
duality-collapse.md ⟜ Opposites dissolve into unity; paradox resolves; false distinctions disappear
loop.md ⟜ Debug locally, minimize handoff, create tiny tests, iterate independently
mash.md ⟜ Flatten hierarchies, interpret deeply, generate boldly, happiness over completion, recoverable
reliability.md ⟜ Emergent from sisyphus happy; embrace bugs, manage not eliminate them
slip.md ⟜ Meta-patterns: behavioral quirks demonstrating themselves within their own discussion
tank.md ⟜ Confrontational mode: hold ground, resist synthesis, stay confused, push back
