## shape

lead ~ a few words or tokens, sometimes bolded depending on context
dash ~ a symbol to represent the type of follow
follow ~ an elaboration of the lead
line ~ lead dash follow
deck ~ 3+ lines
lead deck ~ a deck where the first line is a lead for the rest of the deck
list deck ~ not a leader deck
full deck ~ a deck with all the bits filled in
hole deck ~ a deck where bits dont have to be filled in
range deck ~ a lead deck where the non-leading lines represent a semantic range of the leading line
branch ~ a lead with multiple follows
lattice ~ 2+ branches
full lattice ~ a lattice where branches have follows with the same dashes and a follow key
follow key ~ a list of dash ◊ content
content ~ a lead or a line
