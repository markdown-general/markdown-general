# commons.md

Dualities that our common interests range across are represented by:

**U+25CA (◊)** ⟜ the lozenge

**Unicode** ⟜ Version 1.1 (1993) - over 30 years of support

**Block** ⟜ Geometric Shapes - fundamental and universally needed

**Age** ⟜ ~30 years - mature, well-established character

**Font Support** ⟜ Excellent - included in almost all Unicode fonts

**HTML Entity** ⟜ &loz; or &lozenge; - named entity available

**Common Usage** ⟜ High - familiar in cards, heraldry, geometry

**Rendering** ⟜ Reliable across modern systems and applications

**Fallback** ⟜ Minimal risk - geometric shapes are standard font components

**Visual Metaphor** ⟜ Diamond shape naturally suggests balance and opposition
